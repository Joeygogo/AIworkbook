About myQuickQuiz

This system is a self-test platform that integrates programming and AI, designed to help users review learning content and generate self-assessment questions. You can upload files, preview them, and use AI to generate test questions, making it easier to organize your study materials.

Please be mindful of the copyright guidelines when uploading files and use them primarily for self-learning purposes.

This platform is the Final Project for the CS50 course, and there are still many areas for improvement. For example: the accuracy of the questions, the correctness of the answers, and the support for various document formats, among other aspects.

If you encounter any of the above issues, this platform and its author bear no responsibility. Please review and confirm before using.

Thank you very much.
